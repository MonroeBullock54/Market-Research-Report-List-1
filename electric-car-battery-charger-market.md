<p><p>The electric car battery charger market is primarily divided into two types: on-board and off-board chargers. On-board chargers are installed within the vehicle, converting AC power to the DC needed for the battery, allowing for convenient charging at standard outlets. Off-board chargers, on the other hand, are external charging stations that provide higher charging speeds and are typically found in public charging locations. Both types play a crucial role in supporting the growing electric vehicle infrastructure and promoting adoption.</p></p>
