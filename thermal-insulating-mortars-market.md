<p><p>The thermal insulating mortars market can be classified into two primary types: inorganic insulating mortars and organic insulating mortars. Inorganic insulating mortars, typically composed of materials like cement and silicate, offer durability and high-temperature resistance, making them ideal for various construction applications. On the other hand, organic insulating mortars are based on polymers or resins, providing flexibility and superior thermal performance, often used in modern building designs. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing energy efficiency and thermal comfort in structures.</p></p>
