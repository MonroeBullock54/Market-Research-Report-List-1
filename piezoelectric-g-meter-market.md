<p><p>The Piezoelectric G Meter market comprises three main types based on sensing axes: 1-axis, 2-axis, and 3-axis devices. A 1-axis G meter measures acceleration in a single directional plane, making it suitable for specific applications. The 2-axis variant captures acceleration in two perpendicular directions, providing more comprehensive data. The 3-axis G meter measures acceleration in three dimensions, offering the most detailed analysis, ideal for complex environments such as robotics and aerospace, where multi-directional movement is crucial.</p></p>
