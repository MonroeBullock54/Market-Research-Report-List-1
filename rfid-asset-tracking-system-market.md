<p><strong>The RFID Asset Tracking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active RFID Type</li><li>Passive RFID Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID asset tracking system market is primarily divided into two types: active RFID and passive RFID. Active RFID types utilize battery-powered tags that periodically transmit signals, allowing for real-time tracking over longer distances, making them suitable for high-value assets. Conversely, passive RFID types rely on electromagnetic energy from a reader to activate the tags, offering a cost-effective solution for tracking items over shorter ranges without the need for batteries. Both types enhance inventory management and operational efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
