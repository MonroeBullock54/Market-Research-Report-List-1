<p><ul><li>Polyurethane</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor sealer market features three main types: polyurethane, solvent-based, and water-based sealers. Polyurethane sealers offer strong durability and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Solvent-based sealers provide excellent penetration and protection but can emit strong odors and require proper ventilation during application. Water-based sealers are environmentally friendly, low in VOCs, and easy to apply, though they may require more frequent reapplication. Each type caters to different needs based on performance and environmental considerations.</p></p>
