<p><p>The Smart Radiator Valves market is categorized into two main types: thermostatic valves and electronic valves. Thermostatic valves operate using a bi-metallic strip or wax element to control temperature based on the room's heat, providing a simple yet effective solution for energy efficiency. In contrast, electronic valves employ digital sensors and smart technology for precise temperature control and integration with home automation systems, offering features such as remote access and programmable schedules for enhanced comfort and energy savings.</p></p>
