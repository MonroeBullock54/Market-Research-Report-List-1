<p><p>1,4 Butanediol (BDO) is primarily produced through various processes, each with distinct feedstocks and methods. The Reppe process uses acetylene and formaldehyde, while the Davy process utilizes maleic anhydride as a precursor. The Butadiene process leverages butadiene from petrochemical sources, and the Propylene oxide process uses propylene oxide as a feed. Each method influences BDO's market dynamics regarding production costs, availability, and environmental considerations, shaping its application in industries such as plastics, solvents, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
