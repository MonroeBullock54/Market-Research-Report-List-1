<p><p>A torsional damper is a mechanical device used to reduce or absorb torsional vibrations in rotating systems, such as engines, drivetrains, and industrial machinery. Torsional dampers are commonly used in automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications.</p><p>The Torsional Damper Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.9% during the forecast period. The **Composite Torsional Damper** combines materials like rubber and metal for enhanced strength and vibration control, balancing performance and weight.
