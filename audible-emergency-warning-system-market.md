<p><p>The Audible Emergency Warning System market is segmented into two primary types: Omnidirectional and Rotating Directional. Omnidirectional systems emit sound in all directions, ensuring widespread coverage and alerting individuals in various locations simultaneously. In contrast, Rotating Directional systems focus sound output in a specific direction, allowing for targeted alerts and minimizing unnecessary noise in surrounding areas. Both types play crucial roles in emergency response scenarios, enhancing situational awareness and guiding people to safety during critical incidents.</p></p>
