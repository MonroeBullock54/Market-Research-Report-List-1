<p><p>The medical rubber product market consists of two main types: natural rubber products and artificial rubber products. Natural rubber products, derived from latex, are prized for their elasticity, biocompatibility, and comfort in medical applications like gloves and catheters. In contrast, artificial rubber products, synthesized from petroleum-based sources, offer enhanced durability, chemical resistance, and customization for specific medical uses, such as seals and gaskets. Both types play crucial roles in healthcare, ensuring safety and efficiency in medical devices and protective equipment.</p></p>
