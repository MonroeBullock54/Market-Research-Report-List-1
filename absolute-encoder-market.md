<p><strong>The Absolute Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shafted</li><li>Hollow Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absolute encoder market is categorized into shafted and hollow shaft types. Shafted encoders feature a solid shaft that transmits motion directly from the machinery, providing high precision and reliability in applications like robotics and industrial automation. Hollow shaft encoders, on the other hand, have a central opening, allowing them to fit over existing shafts or components, which simplifies installation and saves space. Both types are critical in applications requiring precise position feedback and are chosen based on specific operational needs.</p></p>
