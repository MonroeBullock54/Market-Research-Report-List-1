<p><p>The ejectors market comprises three primary types: electric, pneumatic, and electromagnetic ejectors. Electric ejectors utilize electric power for operation, offering precise control and efficiency for various applications. Pneumatic ejectors rely on compressed air to create suction, making them ideal for handling lightweight materials and offering flexibility in use. Electromagnetic ejectors use electromagnetic forces to initiate the ejection process, enabling quick response times and reliable performance. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, enhancing material handling and processing efficiency.</p></p>
