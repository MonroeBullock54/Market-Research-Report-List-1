<p><p>The professional hair colors market consists of three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide a short-lived color that typically lasts until the next wash, ideal for those wanting to experiment without commitment. Semi-permanent dyes offer a longer-lasting option, lasting several weeks and gradually fading with washes, suitable for those seeking a more durable change. Permanent hair dyes provide long-lasting color that remains until hair growth or chemical treatment, making it a popular choice for deeper, more consistent results.</p></p>
