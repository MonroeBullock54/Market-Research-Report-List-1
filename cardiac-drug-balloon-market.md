<p><p>The cardiac drug balloon market encompasses two primary types: cardiac glycosides and non-glycosides. Cardiac glycosides, derived from plant sources, enhance heart contractility and improve symptoms in heart failure patients. Non-glycosides, on the other hand, include a broader range of pharmaceuticals that aid in heart rhythm regulation and manage various cardiac conditions without relying on glycoside properties. Both categories play a crucial role in cardiology, addressing diverse patient needs in cardiovascular health management.</p></p>
