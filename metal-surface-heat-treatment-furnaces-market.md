<p><p>Metal surface heat treatment furnaces come in two primary types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical furnaces have a design that allows for efficient treatment of smaller parts, utilizing gravity to enhance heat distribution and facilitate loading. They are often compact and ideal for batch processing. In contrast, horizontal furnaces accommodate larger components and provide uniform heating across the workpiece. This design allows for easier handling of extensive production runs and is often preferred for substantial manufacturing needs, ensuring consistent quality across treatments.</p></p>
