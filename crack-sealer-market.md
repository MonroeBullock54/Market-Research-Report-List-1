<p><strong>The Crack Sealer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Pour Crack Sealer</li><li>Hot Pour Crack Sealer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crack sealer market is primarily divided into two types: cold pour and hot pour sealers. Cold pour crack sealers are ready-to-use products, typically made from asphalt emulsions, designed for quick application and smaller cracks. They cure at room temperature, offering convenience and ease of use. Hot pour crack sealers, on the other hand, require heating to a specific temperature before application, allowing for deeper penetration into cracks. They provide a more durable and long-lasting solution, making them suitable for larger and more extensive crack repairs.</p></p>
