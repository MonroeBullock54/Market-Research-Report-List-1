<p><p>The Flex LED Strip Lights market primarily includes 5050 and 3528 types, distinguished by their size and brightness. The 5050 strips are larger, featuring three diodes per chip, resulting in higher luminosity and better color mixing, making them ideal for vibrant lighting applications. In contrast, the 3528 strips have a smaller size with one diode per chip, offering lower brightness but greater energy efficiency. Both types are widely used in residential and commercial lighting, as well as decorative applications.</p></p>
