<p><p>The MV Circuit Breakers market encompasses three main types: Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breakers (ACB). MCBs are designed for low-voltage overcurrent protection, suitable for residential applications. MCCBs provide enhanced overcurrent protection for industrial applications, featuring adjustable trip settings. ACBs are used for high current applications, providing robust protection in large installations. Each type plays a critical role in electrical safety and circuit protection across various sectors.</p></p>
