<p><strong>The Digital Camera Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ni-MH</li><li>Li-ion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital camera battery market comprises primarily two types: Nickel-Metal Hydride (Ni-MH) and Lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries. Ni-MH batteries are known for their affordability and environmental friendliness, offering decent power capacity and rechargeability. However, they tend to have a higher self-discharge rate. In contrast, Li-ion batteries are favored for their higher energy density, lighter weight, and longer lifespan, making them more suitable for high-performance digital cameras. The choice between them depends on usage requirements and budget considerations.</p></p>
