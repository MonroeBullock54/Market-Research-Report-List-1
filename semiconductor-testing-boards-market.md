<p><ul><li>ProbeCard</li><li>LoadBoard</li><li>Burn-inBoard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor testing boards market comprises three main types: probe cards, load boards, and burn-in boards. Probe cards are utilized for initial testing of semiconductor wafers to ensure functionality at the die level. Load boards are designed for final testing of packaged chips, facilitating the application of signals and power during validation. Burn-in boards are used for reliability testing, exposing chips to elevated temperatures and voltages to identify potential failures early in the product lifecycle. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing semiconductor quality.</p></p>
