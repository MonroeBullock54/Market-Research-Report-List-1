<p><p>Motorcycle sound systems are categorized into two main types: built-in and external. Built-in sound systems are seamlessly integrated into the motorcycle design, offering a sleek aesthetic and reliable performance. These systems often come with advanced features, such as Bluetooth connectivity and weather resistance. On the other hand, external sound systems are standalone units that can be mounted on motorcycles. They provide flexibility in terms of sound quality and upgrades, allowing users to customize their audio experience without altering the bike’s structure.</p></p>
