<p><p>The synthetic molecular sieves market encompasses various types including Zeolite A, Type X, Type Y, Ultra-stable Y (USY), and ZSM-5. Zeolite A is primarily used for water adsorption and detergent applications. Type X and Type Y are effective in catalytic processes and gas separation due to their unique pore structures. USY is a modified version of Type Y, enhancing stability at high temperatures. ZSM-5 is notable for its applications in petroleum refining and petrochemical processes due to its selective catalytic properties.</p></p>
