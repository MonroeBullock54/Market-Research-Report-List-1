<p><ul><li>Electronic Ignition System</li><li>Magneto Ignition System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft ignition system market comprises two primary types: Electronic Ignition Systems (EIS) and Magneto Ignition Systems. EIS utilizes electronic components to generate and control the ignition spark, offering improved performance, fuel efficiency, and reliability. In contrast, Magneto Ignition Systems rely on mechanical components to create electrical energy for ignition, known for their simplicity and robustness, making them suitable for various piston-engine aircraft. Both systems are critical in ensuring optimal engine performance and overall flight safety.</p></p>
