<p><p>The liquid-filled capsules market comprises two primary types: liquid fill hard capsules and liquid fill soft capsules. Liquid fill hard capsules feature a two-piece design that encapsulates liquid formulations, providing a stable delivery method for solid or semi-solid substances. In contrast, liquid fill soft capsules offer a single, flexible shell that allows for encapsulating oils and other liquids, enhancing bioavailability and ease of swallowing. Both types cater to various industries, including pharmaceuticals and dietary supplements, focusing on targeted release and improved absorption.</p></p>
