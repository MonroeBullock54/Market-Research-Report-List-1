<p><ul><li>Open Lifeboats</li><li>Closed Lifeboats</li><li>Freefall Lifeboats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ship lifeboat market comprises three main types: open, closed, and freefall lifeboats. Open lifeboats are traditionally designed for quick evacuation under calm conditions, offering minimal protection from the elements. Closed lifeboats provide enhanced safety and environmental protection, featuring a fully enclosed design that safeguards passengers during harsh weather. Freefall lifeboats are launched by freefalling into the water, allowing for rapid deployment and reduced impact on the vessel's stability, making them ideal for emergencies.</p></p>
