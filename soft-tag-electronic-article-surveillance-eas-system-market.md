<p><p>The Soft Tag Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) System market includes several types of technologies. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) Systems use tags that respond to RFID readers for inventory tracking and theft prevention. Acoustic Magnetic (AM) Anti-Theft Systems utilize magnetic fields to detect tags. Radio Frequency (RF) Systems employ radio waves for tag detection. Electromagnetic Wave (EM) Anti-Theft Systems operate using electromagnetic fields to trigger alarms. Additionally, "Other" market types encompass emerging technologies and hybrid systems that combine features of these established types.</p></p>
