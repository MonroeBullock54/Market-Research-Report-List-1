<p><p>The air filters market encompasses various types designed to enhance air quality across different applications. Pre-filters capture larger particles, extending the life of subsequent filters. Sub-HEPA filters provide moderate filtration, filtering out smaller particles. Secondary filters act as an additional layer, enhancing overall filtration efficiency. HEPA filters are renowned for trapping 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns, while ULPA filters offer even higher efficiency, capturing 99.999% of particles, making them ideal for critical environments like hospitals and cleanrooms.</p></p>
