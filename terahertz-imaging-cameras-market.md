<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Cameras market is primarily divided into two types: passive and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging captures naturally emitted terahertz radiation from objects, making it useful for non-invasive inspection and surveillance applications. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves the use of external terahertz sources to illuminate objects, enhancing contrast and depth perception for detailed analysis. Both technologies serve diverse applications in security, biomedical imaging, and materials characterization, each offering unique advantages in sensitivity and operational flexibility.</p></p>
