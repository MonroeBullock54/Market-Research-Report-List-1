<p><ul><li>Ionic Emulsifier</li><li>Nonionic Emulsifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food grade emulsifying agents facilitate the mixing of water and oils in food products, enhancing texture and stability. The market is categorized into ionic and nonionic emulsifiers. Ionic emulsifiers, such as lecithin and casein, carry a charge, promoting strong interactions in emulsions. Nonionic emulsifiers, like polysorbates and sorbitan esters, are neutral and provide versatility across various food applications. Both types are crucial for achieving desired product consistency, extending shelf life, and improving mouthfeel in diverse food formulations.</p></p>
