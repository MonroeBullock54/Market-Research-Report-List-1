<p><ul><li>Laser Level</li><li>Y-level</li><li>Tilting Level</li><li>Automatic Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The levelling instruments market includes various types catering to different surveying needs. Laser levels provide precise horizontal and vertical alignments using laser beams, ideal for construction projects. Y-levels, or Y-tube levels, are simpler tools that use fluid in a tube to determine evenness. Tilting levels offer a more manual approach for leveling through a telescopic sight, while automatic levels utilize internal compensators to achieve accuracy quickly and efficiently, making them suitable for both professional and DIY applications.</p></p>
