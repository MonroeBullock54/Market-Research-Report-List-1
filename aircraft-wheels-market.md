<p><p>The aircraft wheels market is segmented into two primary types: main wheels and nose wheels. Both types are crucial for overall aircraft performance and safety, influencing design considerations and technology advancements in materials, braking systems, and aerodynamics to enhance efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
