<p><p>The construction carbon fiber fabric market includes various types based on the number of filaments in each tow, such as 3k, 6k, 12k, and others. The "k" denotes thousands of carbon filaments per tow. **3k fabrics** are lightweight and flexible, ideal for intricate designs. **6k fabrics** offer a balance of strength and weight, suitable for structural reinforcement. **12k fabrics** are thicker and stronger, used in heavy-duty applications. **Other types** cater to specialized needs, providing tailored solutions for diverse construction requirements.</p></p>
