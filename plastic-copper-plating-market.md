<p><p>The Plastic Copper Plating market involves applying a copper layer onto plastic surfaces to enhance conductivity, durability, and aesthetics. It includes two main types: **Plating** and **Electroless Plating**. Plating uses an electric current to deposit copper, ideal for high-conductivity applications. Electroless Plating relies on chemical reactions for uniform coating, suitable for complex shapes and non-conductive materials. Both methods are widely used in electronics, automotive, and decorative industries, offering corrosion resistance, improved adhesion, and cost-effective solutions for lightweight, durable components.</p></p>
