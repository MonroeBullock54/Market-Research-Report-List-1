<p><p>Supercontinuum lasers are categorized into two main market types: MIR (Mid-Infrared) lasers and Visible/Near-Infrared (Visible/NIR) lasers. MIR lasers operate in the mid-infrared spectrum, typically used for applications like spectroscopy and medical diagnostics, providing high sensitivity and precision. In contrast, Visible/NIR lasers cover the visible light and near-infrared ranges, widely utilized in imaging, telecommunications, and industrial processes. Both types leverage the nonlinear optical properties of certain materials to generate a broad spectrum of light, enhancing versatility in diverse applications.</p></p>
