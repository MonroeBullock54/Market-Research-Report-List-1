<p><strong>The UVC LED Disinfection Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet Mercury Lamp Disinfection</li><li>UVC-LED Disinfection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UVC LED disinfection robots market primarily features two types of technologies: ultraviolet mercury lamp disinfection and UVC-LED disinfection. Ultraviolet mercury lamps utilize mercury vapor to produce UVC light, effectively killing pathogens but requiring considerable warm-up time and having environmental disposal concerns. In contrast, UVC-LED disinfection employs solid-state technology that allows for instant on/off capabilities, longer lifespan, and lower energy consumption, making it a more environmentally-friendly and versatile option for commercial and healthcare settings.</p></p>
