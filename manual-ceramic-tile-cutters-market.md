<p><ul><li>Single Track Manual Tile Cutter</li><li>Double Track Manual Tile Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual ceramic tile cutters market is divided into two main types: single track and double track manual tile cutters. Single track manual tile cutters feature a single guiding rail, making them suitable for small to medium-sized tiles, offering ease of use and portability. In contrast, double track manual tile cutters incorporate two guiding rails, allowing for greater stability and precision, which is beneficial for larger tiles and achieving clean, straight cuts. Both types cater to varying needs in tile installation projects.</p></p>
