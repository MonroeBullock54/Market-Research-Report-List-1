<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Marine Exhaust Gas Cleaning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Closed Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Hybrid Scrubbers</li><li>Dry Scrubbers</li><li>Membrane Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Exhaust Gas Cleaning System market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Open Loop Scrubbers** use seawater to wash exhaust gases and discharge treated water back into the sea.</p><p>2. **Closed Loop Scrubbers** use a chemical solution and recirculate water, eliminating the discharge of pollutants.</p><p>3. **Hybrid Scrubbers** can operate in both open and closed loop modes, adapting to regulations.</p><p>4. **Dry Scrubbers** employ a solid absorbent to capture pollutants instead of water.</p><p>5. **Membrane Scrubbers** use membrane technology for separation, targeting specific pollutants efficiently.</p></p>
