<p><ul><li>Mechanical scales</li><li>Digital scales</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kitchen scales market is primarily divided into mechanical and digital scales. Mechanical scales use a spring mechanism to measure weight, offering simplicity and reliability without the need for batteries. They are often favored for their durability and ease of use. Digital scales, on the other hand, utilize electronic components for precise measurements, typically featuring LCD displays for easy reading. These scales are popular for their accuracy and additional functions, such as tare weight and unit conversion, appealing to modern cooking needs.</p></p>
