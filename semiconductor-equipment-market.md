<p><ul><li>Semiconductor Front-end Equipment</li><li>Semiconductor Back-end Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor equipment market is divided into two main categories: front-end equipment and back-end equipment. Front-end equipment focuses on manufacturing integrated circuits and involves processes like wafer fabrication, etching, and deposition. This stage transforms raw silicon wafers into functional chips. In contrast, back-end equipment deals with packaging and testing these chips, ensuring their functionality and readiness for integration into various electronic devices. Both segments are crucial for the overall semiconductor production process, catering to different stages of chip development.</p></p>
