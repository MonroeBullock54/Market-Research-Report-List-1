<p><strong>The Thermosensitive Hydrogel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Thermosensitive Hydrogel</li><li>Positive Thermosensitive Hydrogel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermosensitive hydrogel market is categorized into negative and positive thermosensitive hydrogels based on their response to temperature changes. Negative thermosensitive hydrogels shrink in volume when exposed to elevated temperatures, making them suitable for controlled drug delivery and tissue engineering applications. Conversely, positive thermosensitive hydrogels expand as temperatures rise, enhancing their use in wound healing and as scaffolds for cell growth. Both types are essential for developing advanced biomedical applications, offering tailored solutions for various therapeutic needs.</p></p>
