<p><p>The carbon fiber for sports market includes three primary types: PAN-based, viscose-based, and pitch-based carbon fibers. PAN-based carbon fiber is widely used due to its high strength-to-weight ratio and durability, making it ideal for athletic equipment. Viscose-based carbon fiber, derived from regenerated cellulose, offers good flexibility and is often used in textiles. Pitch-based carbon fiber is known for its high thermal and electrical conductivity, suitable for specialized sports applications that require enhanced performance characteristics.</p></p>
