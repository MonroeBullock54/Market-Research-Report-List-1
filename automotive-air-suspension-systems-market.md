<p><ul><li>Manual Air Suspension</li><li>Electronic Air Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air suspension systems market encompasses two main types: manual air suspension and electronic air suspension. Manual air suspension relies on user-operated controls to adjust air pressure in the system, allowing for basic ride height adjustments and load support. In contrast, electronic air suspension utilizes advanced sensors and control systems to automatically adjust the air pressure based on driving conditions, improving ride comfort and vehicle stability. This technology enhances ride quality and handling, appealing to luxury and performance vehicle segments.</p></p>
