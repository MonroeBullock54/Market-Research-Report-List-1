<p><p>The medical polyethylene market primarily consists of two types: Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is known for its flexibility, making it suitable for applications like medical bags and tubing, while HDPE boasts greater strength and chemical resistance, ideal for durable medical equipment and containers.
