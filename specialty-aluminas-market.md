<p><p>The specialty aluminas market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Aluminium trihydroxide (ATH) serves primarily as a flame retardant and filler. Calcined alumina offers high purity for advanced ceramics and abrasives. Tabular alumina is valued for its thermal stability in refractory applications, while fused alumina provides durability and hardness, making it ideal for grinding and abrasive materials.</p></p>
