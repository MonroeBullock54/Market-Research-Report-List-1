<p><p>The polyethylene mulch film market comprises three primary types: LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). LLDPE is known for its flexibility and durability, making it ideal for various agricultural applications. LDPE offers good moisture retention and protection, commonly used in gardening and landscaping. HDPE is tougher and more rigid, suitable for long-lasting agricultural practices. Each type serves specific needs in crop protection, soil warming, and weed control, contributing to enhanced agricultural productivity.</p></p>
