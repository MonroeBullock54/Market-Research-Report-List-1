<p><p>The Helmet Mounted Head Up Display (HUD) market is categorized into two main types: inside the helmet and outside the helmet. Inside helmet HUDs integrate directly into the helmet's visor or display system, providing information to the wearer without obstructing their field of view, ideal for applications in aviation and military. Conversely, outside helmet HUDs project critical data onto a transparent screen positioned in front of the helmet, enhancing situational awareness while allowing for a broader visual perspective in various environments.</p></p>
