<p><strong>The Off-road Vehicles Cooling Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Radiator Fan</li><li>Electric Radiator Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The off-road vehicles cooling fan market comprises two primary types: mechanical radiator fans and electric radiator fans. Mechanical radiator fans, typically driven by the engine’s power, engage automatically based on temperature, delivering reliable cooling without requiring external power. In contrast, electric radiator fans operate independently, using electric motors for enhanced efficiency and adaptability, often controlled by sensors. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal engine temperatures in demanding off-road conditions, ensuring performance and longevity of vehicles in rugged environments.</p></p>
