<p><p>The current measuring devices market includes various types, such as clamp ammeters, probe ammeters, and fork ammeters. Clamp ammeters measure current without disconnecting the circuit, making them ideal for quick assessments. Probe ammeters feature probes that allow direct contact with the circuit for precise measurements. Fork ammeters combine the functionality of clamp meters with a narrow design, allowing for access in tight spaces. Each type serves distinct applications, addressing the needs of professionals in electrical diagnostics and troubleshooting.</p></p>
