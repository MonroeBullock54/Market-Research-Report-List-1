<p><strong>The Tailgate Liner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drop-in Bedliners</li><li>Spray-on Bedliners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tailgate Liner market includes two primary types: Drop-in Bedliners and Spray-on Bedliners. Drop-in Bedliners are pre-molded, durable plastic or rubber liners that fit snugly into the truck bed, offering easy installation and removal. They provide robust protection against scratches and dents. Spray-on Bedliners, on the other hand, are applied as a liquid coating that hardens into a seamless, custom-fit layer. They offer superior durability, resistance to corrosion, and a non-slip surface, making them ideal for heavy-duty use. Both types cater to different customer preferences for truck bed protection.</p></p>
