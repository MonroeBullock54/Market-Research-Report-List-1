<p><p>The Perfluoroalkoxy (PFA) market consists of three primary types: PFA pellets, PFA powder, and PFA aqueous dispersion. PFA pellets are solid granules used primarily in extrusion and molding applications due to their ease of handling and processing. PFA powder serves similar functions but is favored for applications requiring fine surface finishes or coatings. PFA aqueous dispersion consists of fine particles suspended in water, commonly utilized in coatings and sealants for their superior chemical resistance and thermal stability.</p></p>
