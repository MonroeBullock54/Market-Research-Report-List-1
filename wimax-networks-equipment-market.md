<p><p>WiMAX networks equipment market is classified into two main types: Fixed and Mobile. Fixed WiMAX is designed for stationary users, providing robust broadband connectivity over long distances, ideal for rural and underserved areas. It typically requires an antenna and is often used in last-mile applications. Mobile WiMAX, on the other hand, supports mobility, allowing users to connect while in motion. This type offers higher data rates and seamless handover between connections, making it suitable for urban environments and mobile devices.</p></p>
