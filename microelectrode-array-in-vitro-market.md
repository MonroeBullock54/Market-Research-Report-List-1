<p><p>The Microelectrode Array (MEA) in vitro market comprises three main types: Classical MEA, Multiwell-MEA, and CMOS-MEA. Classical MEA features a simple layout of electrodes for cell recording and stimulation. Multiwell-MEA combines multiple classical arrays in a plate format, allowing for high-throughput experimentation. CMOS-MEA utilizes complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, enabling integration of signal processing and higher electrode density, leading to enhanced performance. Each type serves specific research and application needs in neuroscience, pharmacology, and tissue engineering.</p></p>
