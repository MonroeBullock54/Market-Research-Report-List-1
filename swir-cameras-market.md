<p><p>The SWIR (Short-Wave Infrared) camera market is divided into two primary types: SWIR area cameras and SWIR linear cameras. SWIR area cameras capture full images by sensing thermal emissions across a broad spectrum, making them ideal for applications like industrial inspection and security. In contrast, SWIR linear cameras utilize a single line sensor to capture images line-by-line, suited for high-speed scanning tasks such as material sorting and automated inspection processes. Both types leverage the unique advantages of SWIR technology for specialized applications.</p></p>
