<p><p>The DC Linear Variable Displacement Transformer (LVDT) market includes various types based on core mechanisms. Free core LVDTs allow unrestricted movement, offering high sensitivity and precision. Guided core LVDTs feature a constrained core path for improved stability and reduced non-linearity. Sprung core LVDTs incorporate springs to balance core position, enhancing resilience against shock and vibration. Other types may include specialized designs or modifications tailored for specific applications, expanding functional versatility across industries such as aerospace, automotive, and industrial automation.</p></p>
