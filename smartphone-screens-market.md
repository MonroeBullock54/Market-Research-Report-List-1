<p><p>Smartphone screens are categorized into several types, each offering distinct display technologies. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) screens use a backlight and offer good color reproduction but can suffer from limited contrast. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays provide vibrant colors and deeper blacks as each pixel emits its light. AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) is a variant that enhances performance and refresh rates. Other technologies include mini-LED and MicroLED, which aim for improved brightness and energy efficiency, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
