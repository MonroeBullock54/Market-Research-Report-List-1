<p><strong>The Digital Mammography Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Digital Mammography (FFDM)</li><li>Digital Breast Tomography (DBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital mammography machine market primarily comprises two key types: Full Field Digital Mammography (FFDM) and Digital Breast Tomosynthesis (DBT). FFDM utilizes flat-panel detectors to capture high-resolution breast images, enhancing detection accuracy and diagnostic capabilities. In contrast, DBT, also known as 3D mammography, creates multiple overlapping images of the breast, providing a more detailed view that aids in identifying abnormalities hidden in dense tissue. Both technologies aim to improve breast cancer screening and patient outcomes.</p></p>
