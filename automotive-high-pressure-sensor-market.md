<p><p>The automotive high-pressure sensor market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital sensors. Analog type sensors provide continuous voltage or current output that corresponds to varying pressure levels, offering real-time data for immediate monitoring. In contrast, digital type sensors convert pressure readings into digital signals, enabling precise measurements and easier integration with electronic control units. Both types are essential for enhancing vehicle performance, safety, and emissions control, catering to different automotive applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
