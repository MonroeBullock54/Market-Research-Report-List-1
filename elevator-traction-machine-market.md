<p><strong>The Elevator Traction Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geared Machine</li><li>Gearless Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elevator traction machine market is primarily divided into two types: geared and gearless machines. Geared machines use a gearbox to reduce the motor speed, providing effective traction while being cost-efficient for low to mid-rise buildings. Gearless machines, on the other hand, employ a direct-drive system, allowing for smoother and more efficient operation, particularly suited for high-rise structures. These machines offer benefits like lower energy consumption, reduced wear, and quieter performance, influencing their growing adoption in modern elevator systems.</p></p>
