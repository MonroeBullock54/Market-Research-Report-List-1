<p><p>The BR rubber compound market primarily consists of two types: CIIR (Chlorobutyl Rubber) and BIIR (Bromobutyl Rubber). CIIR is known for its excellent air retention and low permeability, making it suitable for tire inner linings and sealants. In contrast, BIIR offers superior heat and ozone resistance, making it ideal for high-performance automotive applications. Both types are vital in industries such as automotive and construction, where durability and performance under varying conditions are critical for end products.</p></p>
