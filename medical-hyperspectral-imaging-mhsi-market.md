<p><p>Medical Hyperspectral Imaging (MHSI) is an advanced imaging technique that captures detailed spectral information from different tissues and materials within the body. This technology is increasingly utilized in surgical guidance, oncology, dermatology, and wound care.</p><p>The MHSI market is expected to experience significant growth, driven by advancements in imaging technology, increasing applications in clinical settings, and a rising prevalence of chronic diseases.
