<p><p>The 2D image sensor market is primarily divided into CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor), CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), and other emerging technologies. CMOS sensors are favored for their low power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for mobile devices and consumer electronics. CCD sensors, known for their superior image quality and sensitivity, are commonly used in professional cameras and scientific applications. Other technologies include organic photodetectors and hybrid sensors, which aim to combine the benefits of both CMOS and CCD sensors.</p></p>
