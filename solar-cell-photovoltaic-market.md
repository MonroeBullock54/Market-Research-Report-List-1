<p><p>The solar cell (photovoltaic) market features several module types. Monocrystalline silicon (Mono-Si) modules, known for their high efficiency and space efficiency, dominate premium segments. Copper Indium Gallium Selenide (CIGS) modules provide flexibility and versatility. Amorphous silicon (a-Si) modules are lightweight and semi-transparent, suitable for various applications. The "Other" category encompasses emerging technologies and niche products.</p></p>
