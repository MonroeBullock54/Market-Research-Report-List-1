<p><strong>The Industrial Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-Mount</li><li>CS-Mount</li><li>F-Mount</li><li>S-Mount</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial lenses market is categorized into several types based on their mount design. C-Mount lenses feature a screw-threaded connection, widely used in industrial cameras. CS-Mount lenses are similar but have a shorter flange distance, making them ideal for compact setups. F-Mount lenses are primarily designed for Nikon cameras and are used in various industrial applications. S-Mount lenses, typically smaller, are suited for space-constrained environments. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses less common mounts, catering to specialized needs.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial lenses market encompasses two primary applications: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture a complete image of a scene in a single exposure, making them suitable for applications like quality control and object recognition. In contrast, line scan cameras capture a one-dimensional line of pixels at a time, useful for inspecting continuous processes like web printing and manufacturing. Both types of cameras rely on high-quality industrial lenses to enhance image resolution and clarity for accurate analysis and detection.</p></p>
