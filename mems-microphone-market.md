<p><ul><li>Analog MEMS Microphone</li><li>Digital MEMS Microphone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS microphone market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital MEMS microphones. Analog MEMS microphones output continuous signals, providing simpler integration into existing audio systems and analog circuits. They are commonly used in applications like consumer electronics and automotive systems. In contrast, digital MEMS microphones convert sound into a digital signal, allowing for enhanced noise reduction and improved sound quality. These are widely utilized in smartphones, smart speakers, and other advanced audio devices, catering to modern connectivity requirements.</p></p>
