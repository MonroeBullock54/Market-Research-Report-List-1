<p><p>The uncoupled dual inductor market is segmented into three main types: radial, SMD/SMT, and through-hole. Radial inductors feature leads extending from one end, suited for space-constrained applications. SMD/SMT inductors are surface-mount devices designed for high-density circuits, offering efficient thermal management. Through-hole inductors have longer leads inserted into PCB holes, providing robust connections in demanding environments. Each type caters to specific design needs, balancing factors such as size, performance, and assembly methods in electronic applications.</p></p>
