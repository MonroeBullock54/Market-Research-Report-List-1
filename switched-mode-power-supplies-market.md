<p><p>The Switched Mode Power Supplies (SMPS) market includes various types of converters that efficiently manage power conversion. DC-DC converters adjust voltage levels for various applications, enhancing device compatibility. Forward converters utilize transformer isolation for improved efficiency and are suitable for medium power levels. Flyback converters offer versatility and simplicity, allowing for multiple output voltages with isolation. Self-oscillating flyback converters further simplify design by using feedback for operation, making them cost-effective for low-power applications. Each type serves distinct needs and applications within the market.</p></p>
