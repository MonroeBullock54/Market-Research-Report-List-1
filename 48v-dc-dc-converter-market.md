<p><p>The 48V DC-DC converter market is segmented into isolated and non-isolated types. Isolated converters use transformers to provide electrical separation between input and output, enhancing safety and noise immunity, making them ideal for sensitive applications. Non-isolated converters, on the other hand, directly convert voltage without electrical isolation, offering simpler designs and higher efficiency for applications where isolation is less critical, such as in distributed power systems or telecommunications. Both types cater to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
