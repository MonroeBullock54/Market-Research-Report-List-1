<p><ul><li>Multi-Turn Encoders</li><li>Single-Turn Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absolute rotary encoders market is divided into two main types: single-turn encoders and multi-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders provide position information within a single rotation, typically covering 360 degrees, making them suitable for applications where angular displacement is limited. Multi-turn encoders, on the other hand, track multiple rotations, offering continuous position data over several turns, ideal for complex automation and robotics applications. This distinction shapes their use in various industries, depending on precision and range requirements.</p></p>
