<p><p>The Peripheral I.V. Catheter (PIVC) market comprises two main types: Short PIVC and Integrated/Closed PIVC. Short PIVC refers to conventional catheters used for short-term intravenous access, designed for quick insertion and immediate usage. In contrast, Integrated/Closed PIVC incorporates features that minimize the risk of contamination, such as built-in connectors or closed systems, enhancing patient safety. Both types cater to diverse clinical settings, ensuring adequate venous access for medication administration and fluid therapy, while addressing infection control concerns.</p></p>
