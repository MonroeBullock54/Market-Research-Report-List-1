<p><p>The stroke treatment drugs market includes various types of medications aimed at preventing and managing strokes. Anticoagulants, such as warfarin and direct oral anticoagulants, reduce blood clot formation. Statins lower cholesterol and stabilize arterial plaques, decreasing stroke risk. Tissue plasminogen activator (tPA) is utilized in acute ischemic strokes to dissolve clots quickly. Antiplatelet drugs, like aspirin, prevent platelets from aggregating, further reducing the likelihood of stroke recurrence. Together, these drug categories enhance stroke management and patient outcomes.</p></p>
