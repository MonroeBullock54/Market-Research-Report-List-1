<p><ul><li>Centralized Dispensing Systems</li><li>Decentralized Dispensing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Medication Dispensing Machines market comprises two primary types: centralized and decentralized dispensing systems. Centralized dispensing systems are typically located in hospital pharmacies, where they manage and dispense medications for multiple departments, enhancing efficiency and control. In contrast, decentralized dispensing systems are placed closer to point-of-care settings, such as patient floors, allowing for immediate access to medications. This proximity improves medication delivery speed and supports better nursing workflows. Both systems contribute to increased safety and accuracy in medication administration.</p></p>
