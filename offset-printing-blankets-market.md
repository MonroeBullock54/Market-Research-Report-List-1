<p><p>The offset printing blankets market includes two main types: compressible blankets and conventional blankets. Compressible blankets feature a cushioned layer that allows for better ink transfer and image quality, making them ideal for high-quality printing applications. Conventional blankets, on the other hand, are firmer and provide stability, suitable for general printing tasks. Both types play a crucial role in offset printing processes, influencing factors such as print consistency, durability, and overall production efficiency.</p></p>
