<p><strong>The Nonwoven Fabrics for Protective Masks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbond Nonwoven</li><li>Meltblown Nonwoven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nonwoven fabrics for protective masks primarily include spunbond and meltblown types. Spunbond nonwovens are made by extruding polymer fibers and bonding them mechanically, providing strength and durability, ideal for mask outer layers. Meltblown nonwovens, produced by melting polymer and blowing it into fine fibers, offer excellent filtration capabilities, making them suitable for the mask's inner layers. Together, these materials enhance mask performance, ensuring comfort, breathability, and effective barrier protection against airborne particles and contaminants.</p></p>
