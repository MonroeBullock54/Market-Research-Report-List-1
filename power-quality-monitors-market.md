<p><p>Power quality monitors are classified into two main market types: on-line and portable. On-line type monitors are permanently installed within electrical systems, offering continuous real-time data on voltage, current, and frequency fluctuations, thereby enabling immediate identification of quality issues. In contrast, portable type monitors are designed for temporary use, allowing users to measure power quality in various locations, making them ideal for troubleshooting or assessments during maintenance. Both types are essential for ensuring efficient and reliable electrical system performance.</p></p>
