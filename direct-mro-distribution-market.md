<p><ul><li>Preventive/Scheduled Maintenance</li><li>Corrective Maintenance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct MRO distribution encompasses two main market types: preventive/scheduled maintenance and corrective maintenance. Preventive maintenance focuses on routine inspections and scheduled servicing to prevent equipment failures and extend asset life. It includes tasks such as lubrication, calibration, and part replacements. In contrast, corrective maintenance addresses unexpected equipment failures, involving repairs or replacements after a breakdown occurs. Both types are crucial for effective maintenance management, ensuring operational efficiency and minimizing downtime in various industries.</p></p>
