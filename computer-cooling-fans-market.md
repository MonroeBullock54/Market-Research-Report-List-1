<p><strong>The Computer Cooling Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer cooling fans market comprises primarily two types: axial fans and centrifugal fans. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan axis, offering efficient airflow in a compact design, making them ideal for general cooling in computer cases. In contrast, centrifugal fans generate airflow at right angles to the intake, producing higher pressure, which is beneficial for cooling components in tighter spaces or specialized applications. Both types are essential for managing heat and ensuring optimal performance in computing systems.</p></p>
