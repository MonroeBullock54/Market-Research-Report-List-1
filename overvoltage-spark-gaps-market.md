<p><p>Overvoltage spark gaps are critical components used for protecting electrical devices from voltage surges. The market is primarily divided into two types: ceramic and metal spark gaps. Ceramic spark gaps offer high insulation resistance and are suitable for high-voltage applications, providing durability and thermal stability. In contrast, metal spark gaps are known for their fast response time and lower voltage breakdown, making them ideal for transient voltage suppression. Each type serves distinct applications depending on environmental conditions and performance requirements.</p></p>
