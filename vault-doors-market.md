<p><ul><li>Out-Swing Vault Doors</li><li>In-Swing Vault Doors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vault doors are categorized primarily into two types: out-swing and in-swing. Out-swing vault doors open outward, making them ideal for tight spaces inside the vault, maximizing usable interior space. They are often favored in high-security environments where emergency exits are crucial. In-swing vault doors, conversely, open inward, offering advantages in security and preventing unauthorized access when closed. This type is commonly used in banks and other financial institutions, providing strong resistance against forced entry while allowing easier access for users.</p></p>
