<p><ul><li>Vacuum</li><li>Non-vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Extrusion Calibration Tank market is segmented into vacuum and non-vacuum types. Vacuum calibration tanks utilize a vacuum environment to maintain precise dimensions and surface quality of extruded products, enhancing control over the cooling process. In contrast, non-vacuum calibration tanks operate without reduced pressure, relying on ambient conditions. While vacuum systems offer superior accuracy ideal for high-precision applications, non-vacuum systems are often more cost-effective and simpler to operate, making them suitable for less demanding production requirements.</p></p>
