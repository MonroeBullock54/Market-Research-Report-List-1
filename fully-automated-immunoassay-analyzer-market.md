<p><p>The Fully Automated Immunoassay Analyzer market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Chemiluminescence Analysis** utilizes light emission from chemical reactions to detect and quantify antigens or antibodies, offering high sensitivity and rapid results.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Fluorescent Immunoassay** relies on fluorescent labels that emit light when excited, enabling precise detection of target molecules with robust signal strength.</p><p>   </p><p>3. **Radioimmunoassay** employs radioactive isotopes to measure antigen-antibody interactions, providing highly sensitive measurements, though usage has declined due to safety and regulatory concerns. </p><p>These techniques cater to various laboratory needs, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
