<p><strong>The Etched Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission Grating</li><li>Reflection Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The etched grating market is primarily divided into two types: transmission gratings and reflection gratings. Transmission gratings allow light to pass through slits or grooves etched into a substrate, optimizing performance in applications like spectroscopy. Reflection gratings, on the other hand, utilize reflective surfaces to redirect light, making them ideal for high-power laser applications. Both types serve critical roles in optical devices, including cameras, lasers, and telecom systems, enabling precise wavelength separation and enhancing imaging capabilities.</p></p>
