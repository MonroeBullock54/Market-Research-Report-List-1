<p><p>The static fluid bed dryer market primarily features two types: continuous and batch systems. Continuous type achieves efficient drying by maintaining a steady flow of material through the dryer, ensuring consistent processing and output over time. In contrast, batch type processes smaller quantities of material in discrete batches, offering flexibility for varying recipes and product changes. Both types cater to different industrial needs, with continuous systems favoring high-volume production and batch systems allowing for greater control over specific drying parameters.</p></p>
