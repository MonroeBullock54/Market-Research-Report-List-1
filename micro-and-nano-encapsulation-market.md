<p><p>The micro- and nano-encapsulation market consists of technologies that encapsulate active substances within microscopic or nanoscopic materials to enhance their stability, functionality, and release profiles. Microencapsulation involves encapsulating particles within a size range of 1 to 1000 micrometers, often used in pharmaceuticals, food, and cosmetics for controlled release and protection. Nanoencapsulation, on the other hand, encapsulates materials at a scale of less than 100 nanometers, offering improved bioavailability and targeted delivery optimized for biomedical applications and advanced material formulations.</p></p>
