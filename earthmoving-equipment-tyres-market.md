<p><strong>The Earthmoving Equipment Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tyres</li><li>Bias Tyres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Earthmoving Equipment Tyres market is primarily divided into two types: radial tyres and bias tyres. Radial tyres feature a casing with layers that radiate from the center, offering improved traction, fuel efficiency, and longevity, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, bias tyres have layers that crisscross, providing greater resilience and puncture resistance but at the cost of fuel efficiency. Each type is tailored to specific operational needs, affecting performance in varied earthmoving tasks.</p></p>
