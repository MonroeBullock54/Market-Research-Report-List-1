<p><p>The silicone surgical cannulae market is segmented into two primary types: curved cannulae and straight cannulae. Curved cannulae are designed for procedures requiring a more angled insertion, enhancing maneuverability and access to difficult anatomical areas. In contrast, straight cannulae provide direct access and are typically used in procedures where precision and linear entry are essential. Both types are crucial in various surgical applications, offering flexibility and adaptability to meet the specific needs of medical professionals during procedures.</p></p>
