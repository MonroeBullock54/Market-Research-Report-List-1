<p><p>Chilled beam systems are advanced HVAC solutions utilizing water to cool indoor spaces. The market is segmented into three main types: concealed, exposed, and recessed. Concealed chilled beams are integrated into the ceiling, providing a sleek aesthetic with minimal visual disruption. Exposed chilled beams are visible and can complement industrial designs while emphasizing functionality. Recessed chilled beams are installed within ceiling spaces, offering a blend of both concealment and accessibility, allowing for efficient cooling without sacrificing design elements.</p></p>
