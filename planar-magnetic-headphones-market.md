<p><p>Planar magnetic headphones use a unique driver technology that combines a flat diaphragm and magnets to create sound. Unlike traditional dynamic headphones, which rely on a movable coil, planar magnetic designs distribute sound evenly across the diaphragm, resulting in clearer audio and a more accurate soundstage.
