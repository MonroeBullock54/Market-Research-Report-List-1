<p><strong>The Temperature Probes/Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Temperature Sensors</li><li>Contact Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature probes/sensors market is categorized into two main types: non-contact and contact temperature sensors. Non-contact temperature sensors, such as infrared sensors, measure temperature from a distance by detecting emitted thermal radiation, making them ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. Contact temperature sensors, including thermocouples and resistance temperature detectors (RTDs), require physical contact with the object to provide accurate readings, widely used in industrial applications for precise temperature monitoring and control. Both types serve critical roles across various industries.</p></p>
