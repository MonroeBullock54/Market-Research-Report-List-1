<p><p>The Breast MRI Screening System market is categorized into two main types: closed systems and open systems. Closed systems are enclosed machines that provide higher magnetic field strength and superior imaging quality, making them ideal for detailed assessments. In contrast, open systems offer a more patient-friendly experience due to their wider openings and reduced claustrophobia, although they may have lower field strength and resolution. Both systems cater to different patient needs and clinical requirements, driving their respective adoption rates in medical facilities.</p></p>
