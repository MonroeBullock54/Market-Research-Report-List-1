<p><p>The Severe Breathing Care Ventilator market is divided into two main types: invasive and non-invasive ventilators. Invasive ventilators involve the placement of a tube into the patient's airway, providing direct airflow and support, often used in critical care settings. Non-invasive ventilators deliver air through masks or similar interfaces, facilitating breathing assistance without needing intubation. Both types are essential for managing respiratory failure, with applications in hospitals, home care, and emergency situations, catering to varying patient needs and comfort levels.</p></p>
