<p><p>The handset proximity sensor market includes various types, primarily focusing on optical and ultrasonic displacement sensors. Optical displacement sensors use light to detect the presence of nearby objects by measuring changes in the light pattern. In contrast, ultrasonic displacement sensors utilize sound waves to gauge proximity, providing accurate measurements regardless of lighting conditions. Both types are essential for enhancing user experience, enabling features such as automatic screen dimming and wake functions, thereby contributing to the overall functionality of handheld devices.</p></p>
