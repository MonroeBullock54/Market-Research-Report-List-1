<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fiber laser market is primarily divided into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and Pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers provide a constant beam output, ideal for applications requiring steady cutting or welding, achieving higher efficiency in heat delivery. In contrast, pulsed fiber lasers emit energy in short bursts, making them suitable for precise, intricate tasks like marking and engraving. Both types serve diverse industries, enhancing productivity and quality in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
