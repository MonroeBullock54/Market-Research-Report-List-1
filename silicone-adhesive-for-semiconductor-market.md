<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone adhesives for the semiconductor market are categorized into single-component and two-component types. Single-component silicone adhesives are pre-cured formulations that require no mixing, providing ease of use and faster application processes. They offer good flexibility and thermal resistance, making them suitable for various semiconductor applications. In contrast, two-component silicone adhesives require a mixing process before application, resulting in enhanced bond strength and curing properties. This type is often preferred for demanding applications that require precise adhesion and performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>
