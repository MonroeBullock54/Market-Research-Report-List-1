<p><strong>The Industrial 3D Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial 3D Camera Market comprises primarily two types: Area Scan Cameras and Line Scan Cameras. Area Scan Cameras capture entire images at once, ideal for static or slow-moving objects, providing rich detail and depth perception. In contrast, Line Scan Cameras capture images one line at a time, suited for high-speed applications where continuous, long objects need inspection, such as in manufacturing and quality control. Both types enable enhanced automation and precision in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
