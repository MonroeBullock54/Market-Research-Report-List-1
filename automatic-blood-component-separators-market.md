<p><p>Automatic Blood Component Separators are specialized devices designed to separate blood into its individual components, such as red blood cells, plasma, and platelets. The market is segmented into two primary types: Single Separation Path and Double Separation Path. Single Separation Path systems can isolate one type of blood component at a time, making them simpler and often more affordable. In contrast, Double Separation Path systems can simultaneously process and separate two components, enhancing efficiency and offering better yield for multiple products during a single operation.</p></p>
