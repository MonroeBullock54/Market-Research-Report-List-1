<p><ul><li>Electrically Conductive Adhesives</li><li>Non-Electrically Conductive Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic assembly adhesives market is divided into two main types: electrically conductive adhesives and non-electrically conductive adhesives. Electrically conductive adhesives are designed to create electrical connections between components, commonly used in applications like circuit boards and LED assemblies. In contrast, non-electrically conductive adhesives are used primarily for bonding and securing components without the need for electrical conductivity, making them suitable for insulation and protection in various electronic devices. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing performance and reliability in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
