<p><p>The Pickup Wheel Speed Sensor market is categorized into two main types: Hall Effect Sensors and Magnetic Electric Sensors. Hall Effect Sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel speed, offering high accuracy and durability, making them ideal for modern vehicles. In contrast, Magnetic Electric Sensors operate based on electromagnetic principles, providing reliable performance in various driving conditions. Both types play crucial roles in automotive systems, enabling effective traction control and anti-lock braking by accurately measuring wheel rotation speed.</p></p>
