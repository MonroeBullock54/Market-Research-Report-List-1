<p><p>Airport passenger boarding steps are essential for facilitating the loading and unloading of passengers onto aircraft. The market is divided into two main types: towable steps and self-propelled steps. Towable types are moved by a separate vehicle and are typically more cost-effective, suitable for smaller airports. Self-propelled types, on the other hand, have their own engine, allowing for greater mobility and efficiency, ideal for larger airports with high passenger traffic. Both types enhance operational efficiency at airports.</p></p>
