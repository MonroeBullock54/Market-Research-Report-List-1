<p><strong>The AS-Interface Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AS-i Gateway/Master</li><li>AS-i Power Supply</li><li>AS-i Slave</li><li>AS-i Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AS-Interface market comprises several key components essential for automation networks. The AS-i Gateway/Master facilitates communication between AS-Interface networks and higher-level systems, acting as a bridge. AS-i Power Supplies provide the necessary power to the network, ensuring reliable operation. AS-i Slaves are field devices that connect to the AS-Interface, executing commands and sending back data. Lastly, AS-i Cables are specialized wiring used for connecting these devices, enabling effective data transmission and power distribution across the network.</p></p>
