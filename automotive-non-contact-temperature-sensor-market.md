<p><p>The automotive non-contact temperature sensor market includes various technologies: </p><p>1. **Thermistor**: Temperature-sensitive resistors used for accurate temperature measurement.</p><p>2. **Resistance Temperature Detector (RTD)**: Utilizes the resistance change in metals to gauge temperature, offering high precision.</p><p>3. **Thermocouple**: Junction of two different metals generates a voltage correlated to temperature, suitable for diverse applications.</p><p>4. **IC Temperature Sensor**: Integrated circuits providing precise temperature readings for compact applications.</p><p>5. **MEMS Temperature Sensor**: Micro-electro-mechanical systems for miniaturized, efficient temperature sensing.</p><p>6. **Infrared Sensor**: Measures thermal radiation without contact, ideal for quick surface temperature assessments.</p></p>
