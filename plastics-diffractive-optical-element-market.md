<p><strong>The Plastics Diffractive Optical Element Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beam Shaping (Top-Hat)</li><li>Beam Splitting</li><li>Beam Foci</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastics diffractive optical element (DOE) market comprises three primary types: Beam Shaping (Top-Hat), Beam Splitting, and Beam Foci. Beam Shaping (Top-Hat) DOEs create uniform light distributions, ideal for applications requiring even illumination. Beam Splitting DOEs divide a beam into multiple paths, useful in imaging and sensor technologies. Beam Foci DOEs concentrate light into specific focal points, enhancing performance in laser systems. Each type caters to diverse industries, including telecommunications, display technologies, and biomedical applications.</p></p>
