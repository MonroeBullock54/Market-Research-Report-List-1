<p><p>The automotive standalone HVAC market is divided into two main types: manual HVAC and automatic HVAC systems. Manual HVAC systems require users to adjust temperature and airflow settings manually, providing a more hands-on experience. In contrast, automatic HVAC systems utilize sensors and advanced controls to automatically regulate cabin climate, enhancing passenger comfort with minimal intervention. The choice between these systems can affect vehicle design, manufacturing complexity, and overall user experience in managing cabin temperature.</p></p>
