<p><p>The aircraft interior fasteners market comprises two main types: threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, including screws and bolts, provide strong connections and allow for easy assembly and disassembly, making them ideal for various interior components. Non-threaded fasteners, such as rivets and clips, offer a lightweight solution and are often used for permanent attachments, ensuring structural integrity without the need for threading. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety, reliability, and efficiency in aircraft interior design and maintenance.</p></p>
