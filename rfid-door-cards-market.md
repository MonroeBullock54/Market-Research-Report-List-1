<p><p>The RFID Door Cards market comprises primarily three types: Proximity Cards, Contactless Smart Cards, and Others. Proximity Cards are designed for short-range communication, usually requiring close proximity to the reader for access. Contactless Smart Cards utilize advanced encryption and offer enhanced security features, enabling seamless user authentication over longer distances. The "Others" segment includes various specialized RFID cards such as dual-interface cards or those with additional functions, catering to diverse applications in access control and security systems.</p></p>
