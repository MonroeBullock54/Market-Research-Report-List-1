<p><p>The Bromide Ion Selective Electrodes market is categorized into three main types: Crystal Membrane, Liquid Membrane, and PVC Membrane. Crystal Membrane electrodes utilize solid-state crystal structures, offering stable and accurate measurements. Liquid Membrane electrodes rely on a liquid ion-exchange phase to detect bromide ions, often providing enhanced selectivity. PVC Membrane electrodes incorporate polyvinyl chloride for ion exchange, balancing durability with performance. Each type serves specific applications in environmental monitoring, water quality assessment, and various industrial processes.</p></p>
