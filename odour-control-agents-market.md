<p><p>Odour control agents are crucial in managing unwanted smells in various industries. The adsorption control agent market involves materials that capture and hold odour molecules on their surfaces, effectively reducing their presence in the air. Common examples include activated carbon and zeolites. In contrast, the oxidation control agent market focuses on chemical compounds that neutralize odours by facilitating oxidation reactions, transforming offensive substances into less harmful or odourless compounds. Together, these technologies enhance air quality and improve environmental conditions.</p></p>
