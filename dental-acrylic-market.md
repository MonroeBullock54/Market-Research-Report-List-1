<p><ul><li>Heat Cure Acrylic Resin</li><li>Self-Curing Acrylic Resin</li><li>Cold Cure Acrylic Resin</li><li>Light-Cured Acrylic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental acrylic market comprises several types, each tailored for specific applications. Heat cure acrylic resin is known for its durability and is commonly used in prosthetics. Self-curing acrylic resin hardens at room temperature and is easy to manipulate, ideal for temporary restorations. Cold cure acrylic resin offers rapid setting, making it suitable for chairside applications. Light-cured acrylic resin is activated by UV light, providing precise control and minimal heat generation, making it favored for modern dental practices.</p></p>
