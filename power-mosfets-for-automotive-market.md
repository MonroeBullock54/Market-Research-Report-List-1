<p><p>Power MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors) are crucial in automotive applications for efficient switching and amplification of electrical signals. N-channel MOSFETs, favored for their high efficiency and robustness, are often used in power management systems and motor drives. P-channel MOSFETs, while less common, are utilized in high-side switching applications. Dual-channel MOSFETs combine both N-channel and P-channel in a single package, providing versatility and space-saving solutions, ideal for integrated vehicle control systems and enhancing overall performance in automotive electronics.</p></p>
