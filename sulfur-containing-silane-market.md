<p><ul><li>Polysulfide Type</li><li>Sulfhydryl Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sulfur-containing silane market includes two primary types: polysulfide type and sulfhydryl type. The polysulfide type features silanes with multiple sulfur atoms, enhancing properties like adhesion and flexibility in applications such as sealants and coatings. In contrast, the sulfhydryl type contains thiol (-SH) groups, which improve bonding and reactivity in both organic and inorganic materials. These types are essential in industries like construction, automotive, and electronics, providing specialized solutions for enhanced performance and durability.</p></p>
