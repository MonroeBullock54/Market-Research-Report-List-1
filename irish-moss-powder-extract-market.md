<p><p>The Irish Moss Powder Extract market encompasses three main types of carrageenan: Kappa, Iota, and Lambda. Kappa carrageenan is primarily used as a gelling agent, forming firm gels in food products. Iota carrageenan provides softer gels, ideal for dairy and dessert applications. Lambda carrageenan does not gel but thickens and stabilizes liquids, making it valuable in sauces and soups. Each type has unique properties suited for different sectors, including food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
