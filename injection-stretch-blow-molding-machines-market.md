<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Type</li><li>Two-Stage Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injection stretch blow molding machines are categorized into two primary types: single-stage and two-stage systems. Single-stage machines perform injection molding and blow molding in one continuous process, allowing for higher efficiency and reduced cycle times, making them ideal for small production runs. In contrast, two-stage machines separate the injection and blow molding processes, allowing for greater flexibility and scalability for larger production volumes. Each type is suited for different production requirements, material types, and product designs in the market.</p></p>
