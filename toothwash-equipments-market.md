<p><p>The toothwash equipment market predominantly features two types: ultrasonic scaling and manual scaling. Ultrasonic scaling utilizes high-frequency vibrations to remove plaque and tartar efficiently, providing a thorough cleaning while minimizing discomfort. In contrast, manual scaling involves handheld instruments operated by dental professionals, allowing for precise control in areas where ultrasonic devices may not reach effectively. Both methods aim to enhance oral hygiene, with ultrasound offering speed and ease, while manual scaling provides meticulous attention to detail in dental care.</p></p>
