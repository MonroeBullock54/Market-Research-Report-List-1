<p><strong>The Pneumatic Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Quarter-Turn Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic actuator market is primarily categorized into single-acting and quarter-turn actuators. Single-acting pneumatic actuators use air pressure to perform movement in one direction, relying on a spring mechanism for return, making them efficient for applications needing minimal force. In contrast, quarter-turn actuators facilitate 90-degree rotational movement, ideally suited for valves and dampers, ensuring precise control. Both types are valued for their reliability, speed, and applicability across various industries such as oil and gas, manufacturing, and water management.</p></p>
