<p><p>The N95 protective mask filter material market primarily features two types: polypropylene (PP) nonwoven and polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) nonwoven. PP nonwoven material is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness, lightweight properties, and efficient filtration capabilities, effectively trapping particles. In contrast, PTFE nonwoven offers superior breathability and enhanced filtration efficiency, making it ideal for specialized applications. Both materials play vital roles in ensuring the masks meet health and safety standards while providing comfort and protection to users.</p></p>
