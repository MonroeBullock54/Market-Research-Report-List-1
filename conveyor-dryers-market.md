<p><p>The conveyor dryers market is primarily categorized into conveyor belt dryers and infrared conveyor dryers. Conveyor belt dryers utilize a continuous moving belt to transport products through a drying tunnel, ensuring efficient moisture removal for various materials. In contrast, infrared conveyor dryers employ infrared radiation to directly heat the surface of products, speeding up the drying process. Both types are widely used in industries such as food processing, textiles, and pharmaceuticals, offering advantages in energy efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
