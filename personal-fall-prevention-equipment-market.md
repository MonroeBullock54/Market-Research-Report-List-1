<p><p>The personal fall prevention equipment market comprises various essential types. Safety harnesses secure the user, distributing fall forces across the body. Safety lanyards connect workers to anchor points, absorbing shock during falls. Anchorage connectors facilitate secure attachment to stable structures. Fall arresters automatically lock during a fall, providing instant safety. Lifeline systems enable safe movement along a fixed path while preventing free falls. Other equipment includes helmets, impact vests, and safety glasses, enhancing overall fall protection in various industrial settings.</p></p>
