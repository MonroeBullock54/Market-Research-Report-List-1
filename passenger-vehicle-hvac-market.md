In contrast, Automatic HVAC systems use sensors and algorithms to maintain desired temperature settings automatically, offering enhanced comfort and convenience. As vehicle technology evolves, the demand for Automatic HVAC is increasing due to its efficiency and user-friendly features, while Manual HVAC remains prevalent in more budget-friendly models.</p></p>
