<p><p>The Outdoor Air Quality Monitoring Device market is categorized into several types based on monitoring techniques. Active/Continuous Monitoring involves real-time data collection using automated sensors. Passive Monitoring relies on absorbent materials to capture pollutants over time without active pumps. Intermittent Monitoring conducts regular measurements at set intervals, while Manual Monitoring requires human intervention for data collection. Stack Monitoring focuses on emissions from industrial stacks, assessing the air quality impact of specific sources. Each method serves unique applications and requirements in air quality assessment.</p></p>
