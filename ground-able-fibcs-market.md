<p><p>Ground-able Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs) are classified based on thread spacing, impacting their static electricity dissipation properties. The less than 20mm thread spacing category features tighter woven fabrics, enhancing conductivity and preventing static build-up in sensitive applications. The 20mm-50mm range offers a balance between strength and conductivity, suitable for general bulk material handling. Above 50mm thread spacing focuses on sturdiness for heavy-duty applications, though with a higher static risk, making proper grounding essential during use.</p></p>
