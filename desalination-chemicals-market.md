<p><p>The desalination chemicals market comprises various types, including inhibitors, defoamers, and fungicides. Inhibitors prevent scale formation and corrosion in desalination systems, enhancing operational efficiency. Defoamers reduce foam buildup during the desalination process, improving productivity and equipment lifespan. Fungicides are used to control microbial growth, ensuring water quality and system integrity. Together, these chemicals optimize desalination processes, minimize maintenance costs, and improve overall performance in water treatment facilities.</p></p>
